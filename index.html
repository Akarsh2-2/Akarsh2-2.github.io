About Me

My academic and professional journey has been a blend of adaptability, perseverance, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. Starting from a Hindi medium background from the UP Board, I made a significant transition to the English medium to the CBSE Board for my 12th grade. This shift not only broadened my linguistic skills but also prepared me for the challenges ahead.

With a strong foundation in academics, I embarked on the rigorous journey of preparing for JEE Mains and JEE Advanced. My efforts led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from G.B. Pant University of Agriculture & Technology, Pantnagar. This phase of my life was instrumental in honing my technical skills and deepening my understanding of the engineering world.

After graduation, I joined Reliance, where I spent 2.5 years working in Delhi. My experience at Reliance was transformative, as I navigated through complex projects, collaborated with diverse teams, and contributed to significant advancements in the field. This role solidified my interest in the intersection of technology and management.

Eager to further expand my horizons, I am currently pursuing an M.Tech in Product Design & Management from IIIT Hyderabad. This program is allowing me to merge my technical expertise with strategic thinking, equipping me with the tools to innovate and drive impactful product solutions.
